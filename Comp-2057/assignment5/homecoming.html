Spider-Man: Homecoming

Spidey senses are tingling!

Spider-Man: Homecoming, the movie to develop Spider-Man as a suitable part of the Avengers. This movie introduced the mentorship of Peter Parker and Tony Stark. This film's main antagonist was the Vulture who almost caught Peter off guard and almost took him down, however through Stark's intervention of this conflict provoked Vulture to turn to Peter as a main target to disassemble the Avengers. This assumption was due to the fact that Tony helped Peter during his most dire moment. Thus, introduced the main conflict of this film. Additionally, this film further developed the topic of, "With great power comes great responsibilities". This was mostly due to the fact that Peter was not careful in the usage of his new found suit built by Tony Stark. This movie built up the character of Spider-Man to meet the Avenger's standards, even though he ended up rejecting the idea of joining the Avengers.
