The Avengers

The Beginning of the Story

In the original Avengers movie introduces the original superheros. Those are; Tony Stark's Iron, Steve Roger's Captain America, Bruce Banner's Hulk, Clint Barton's Hawkeye, Thor Odinson's Thor, and Natasha Romanoff's Black Widow. Along with introducing the original characters, you will see a neutral character by the name of Loki. Loki shows up in the main Avengers movies such as; Avengers, Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame. His role as a neutral is significant enough as he proposes the multiple instances of hardships the other superheros have to overcome. This movie was significant in creating the foundations of the Avengers and some neutral characters, namely; Loki, and Nick Fury. The conflict generated in this film would be later used to set up the post-traumatic stress disorder that Tony Stark will be experiencing in later movies.
