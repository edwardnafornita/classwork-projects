Avengers: Age of Ultron

Introduction of the Robot(s)

In Avengers: Age of Ultron viewers are introduced to a new hero and villain. The two robots, Ultron and Vision, showcase new difficulties and new responsibilities for our main heros. Ultron's motive, being the villain that was introduced in this movie, was initially to protect and serve the community, however through a glitch in his system and Ultron learning about the history of humans and witnessing how dangerous humans are to the world. This created the villain of Ultron, as his artificial intelligence altered the mindset of Ultron to erradicate humans in attempt to save the planet from themselves.

After the events that caused Ultron's motive to change, he created a new android known as Vision to aid Ultron. However, through the efforts of the Avengers and after 6 years of time, Vision was created to become a hero who wields the Mind stone. This artifact then became a massive responsibilities for the Avengers.
