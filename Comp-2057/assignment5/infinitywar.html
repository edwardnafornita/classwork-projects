Avengers: Infinity War

The Beginning of the End

In this film we begin the sequence of events that will bring the Avengers films to a close. The main antagonist that was introduced in the Guardians of the Galaxy known as Thanos will put his mission to annihilate the planet Earth in motion. His main motives for doing this is due to his nature of destruction and thrill for power. Thanos is infamously known for his hunger for destruction, as he uses his army to erradicate other nations in turn creating a powerful nation of his own. Thanos' plan involves him gathering six powerful artifacts known as Infinity Stones.

Each Infinity Stone has its own Significance

  1. Power Stone: Enhances the strength and allows the user to manipulate all forms of energy.
  2. Time Stone: Part of the Eye of Agamotto, allows the user to manipulate time and see in the future and the past.
  3. Mind Stone: Grants the user the ability to control the hearts and minds of others.
  4. Reality Stone: Provides the user the ability to manipulate reality itself and create any vision the user may have at will.
  5. Space Stone: Grants the user the ability to create a portal to any location in the Universe.
  6. Soul Stone: Allows the user to possess souls of living and dead entities. It is known to be the strongest Infinity Stone in the entire universe.


Once Thanos has all of these stones collected in his Infinity Gauntlet, (a gauntlet with indentations which are perfectly engineered to fit each infinity stone), he can then utilize all infinity stones simultaneously to bend space, time and reality itself and mould it into whatever he desires. All with just a snap.
In this case, the way Infinity War comes to a close was through the snap of the infinity gauntlet to erradicate half of the entire universe. This sets the foundations for Avengers: Endgame.
