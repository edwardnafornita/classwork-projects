Black Panther

The Wakandan King

The Black Panther Movie brings forth the incredible Black Panther and his story about his rise to power. Throughout this movie, we learn the hardships of T'Challa from learning the life of a generic human to becoming the king of Wakanda. With this rise to power comes conflict amongst the antagonists of this film. That being the faction of the Killmonger. Killmonger provokes T'Challa into starting a war between the nations in Wakanda, such that Killmonger can claim the title of King of Wakanda. T'Challa chooses to compete against this rival of his all for the sole purpose of avoiding a full-scale war on and potentially the fall of this african nation. After teaming up with previous ally Shuri and a CIA agent, Black Panther is tasked with reclaiming his title as king alongside sparing as many lives in his nation as possible. The end of this film shows Black Panther reclaiming his title, whilst also granting him the recognition and respect as the proper heir to the throne and as a Wakandan warrior. T'Challa then chooses to establish a connection with the Avengers and acknowledges them as a good ally. This will be a very important event in the Marvel Cinematic Universe as we approach the topics of Infinity War and Endgame which will tie up the loose ends of the story.
