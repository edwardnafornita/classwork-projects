Doctor Strange

The Interdemensional Beings

In Doctor Strange we get introduced to a famous neurosurgeon who ended up getting into a near fatal car crash rendering him incapable of using his hands. This taught the egotistical doctor to appreciate all the people around him as before, all he cared about was himself alone. Later, Steven Strange learned the art of mystical arts where he met Wong and the Sorcerer Supreme after being stranded for many hours in a snowy mountain. Strange learned all about the mystical arts and his curiosity led him astray and stumbled upon the Eye of Agamotto. In which, he discovered how to utilize the eye and bend the fabric of reality.

Fast forward through some fights and the correction of time itself, Doctor Strange is now stable with the Eye of Agamotto in tact. He was approached by the Avengers to utilize his abilities to aid the Avengers in saving the world.
