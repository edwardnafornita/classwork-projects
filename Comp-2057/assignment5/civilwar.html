Captain America: Civil War

The Division of Heros

Captain America Civil War shows the audience how the greatest heros of all time, can be conflicted through their own morals. In this film, Steve Rogers (Captain America) finds himself bashing skulls with Tony Stark. Each side contains their own faction led by their respective leaders. The problems occurred when Tony disagreed with the way Rogers managed his faction. This sparked the feud between the two and hence, began the Civil War. This was an important part of the MCU since it created conflict that was later resolved through the realisation of Rogers. He dropped his ego and made ammends to Stark as if that didn't occur the potential for mass destruction of the Avengers would've occurred.
