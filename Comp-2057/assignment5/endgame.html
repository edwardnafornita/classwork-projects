Avengers: Endgame

The Ending Everyone Wanted

This film closes off the entirety of Avengers. The current state of the world starts off with where Thanos erradicated half of the Universe. This means that half of the Avengers are gone, and the other half are stunned about what their next steps are going to be. As it stands, Thanos decided that after erasing half of the Universe, he should destroy the Infinity Stones so that no one will be able to undo what he worked so hard to obtain (that being a universe that he rules). Five years pass as the disassembled Avengers struggle to find out what to do, they branch off and start living their lives like any normal folk. Some Avengers stay and provide help to Law Enforcements, while others have choosen to live their own life. Tony Stark in particular is now married to Pepper Potts, and they reside in a cabin. Whereas, the Romanoff chose to help Law Enforcements until they chose to persue the resurrection of their fallen allies.

The Time Machine
Romanoff chose to interrogate Tony Stark to create a way to manipulate space-time and traverse back into the past to collect the infinity stones before Thanos is able to erase half of the universe. Although Stark denies helping them numerous times, he finally gave in when he pulls a past photo of his former subordinate Peter Parker. Later he finds the solution to create a space-time GPS to traverse back into the past.
The Time Heist
With the new space-time GPS, whatever remains of the Avengers choose to go back in time to put their plan into motion of essentially playing a cat vs mouse game with Thanos. The team pinpoints the locations of all the infinity stones in the past and split into three separate teams tasked with collecting their infinity stones in their respective time periods. The way each team was decided was through timeline specifics. For example, Hydra happened during the time of Captain America, Iron Man, and the Hulk, so they split apart to find the Space, Time and Mind stones. The Reality stone was tasked to Thor since it resides in Asgard, and then all that was left was to find the Power and Soul stones as last and the locations for those are on a separate planets known as Morag and Vormir.
The Resurrection
After the Avengers found all the infinity stones, they teleported back to the present and the Avengers have all the stones in their possession. The only problem is that they have to go back in time to prevent Thanos from erasing half of the universe. The only way to do this is to kill Thanos before he can snap his fingers. When they teleported back to that time, Thanos found them and caught them off guard as Thanos speculated a scenario like this might happen. So as a response from him he sent his underlings to retrieve the stones from the Avengers. The Avengers were able to fend off the underlings, but they were out matched by Thanos. Thanos was able to get the stones, and mentioned the inevitability of his actions in which Tony Stark quickly swapped the stones from Thanos' gauntlet and placed them on his own gauntlet. In turn, when Thanos went to snap his fingers and realised that nothing occurred, he peered to Stark who now has all of the stones. Thanos then went to attack Stark, but was stopped by Captain America who was able to stab Thanos with his shield. Stark then snapped his fingers and erased the stones from existance. Stark met the same fate as Thanos when he tried to elliminate the stones, however, the Avengers were able to bring back the fallen Avengers alongside the other half of the universe. This was the end of the Infinity Saga and the end of the Avengers movies.
