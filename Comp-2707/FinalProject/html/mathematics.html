Advanced Mathematics for Computer Science

Instructor: Karen Johnson

Course Content:

  • - Discrete Mathematics: Set theory, logic, relations, functions, and graph theory.
  • - Linear Algebra: Vectors, matrices, eigenvalues, eigenvectors, and linear transformations.
  • - Calculus: Limits, differentiation, integration, sequences, and series.
  • - Probability and Statistics: Probability theory, random variables, distributions, and hypothesis testing
  • - Numerical Analysis: Approximation, interpolation, numerical integration, and iterative methods.
  • - Optimization: Linear programming, convex optimization, and non-linear optimization.
  • - Combinatorics: Permutations, combinations, and counting principles.
